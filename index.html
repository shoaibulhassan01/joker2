Pepe Joker Token Distribution

Pre-Sale (20%):

A set amount reserved for early backers and initial supporters, offering them a chance to get in before the public sale, typically at a discounted rate.

Public Sale (15%):

Open to the general public, this allows a broader set of investors to purchase the tokens, often marking the project's official launch.

Team & Founders (15%):

Allocated to the core team and founders. This is usually vested over a period (e.g., 2-4 years) to ensure long-term commitment and alignment of interests.

Reserve Fund (10%):

Reserved for unforeseen circumstances, emergencies, or opportunities that might arise in the future.

Community Rewards & Airdrops (10%):

Distributed to the community as rewards for various activities such as promotions, competitions, and engagements that help in growing the ecosystem.

Development & Maintenance (10%)

Dedicated to ongoing development, technological advancements, maintenance, and updates to ensure the token remains competitive and up-to-date.

Marketing & Promotion (5%)

Allocated for marketing campaigns, promotions, influencer partnerships, and other activities to enhance visibility and adoption.

Advisors (3%):

For mentors, industry experts, and professionals who provide valuable insights, connections, and direction to the project.

Charity & Social Initiatives (2%):

A commitment to give back, this portion is reserved for charitable contributions or to support causes aligned with the ethos of Pepe Joker.
